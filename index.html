DNS Neutrality

No corporation should say what websites should or shouldn’t be available to us, or the speed at which they load. By using OpenNIC you no longer have to question your ISPs motives, and can rest assured that your connection to the Internet is not being censored by your DNS servers.

No Cost

OpenNIC is a non profit organization and does not charge money for access to its DNS services including the proposal/request of new TLDs. It’s free to use completely operated by volunteers, so there’s no financial pressure to corrupt the OpenNIC system. You too could volunteer and support the system!

Stop DNS Hijacking

Have you ever typed a wrong URL into your browser only to be met with an ISP-owned search page? The domain you tried to visit, ads you click, and the searches you do can all be collected by your ISP for data-mining and marketing operations. You can stop this behavior with OpenNIC's servers, which lets DNS work the way it was designed to: in your control.
